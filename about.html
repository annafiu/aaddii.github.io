Architect & Writer Adi Iman Wicaksono

About Adi Iman Wicaksono

I believe design should be critical, contextual, conscious, and efficient; above all, it must make sense.

I am an Architect at Tamara Wibowo Architects based in Semarang, Indonesia. My professional practice spans from early preliminary design proposals to overseeing construction projects, covering everything from interior elements to public building scales. I am committed to establishing efficient project workflows with a detail-oriented approach.

Beyond the Drafting Table

While my professional focus lies in architectural practice, I am equally passionate about the discourse surrounding architecture and art. I have extensive experience in critical writing, architectural publishing, and historical research.

My editorial experience includes serving as the Managing Editor for PALIMPSEST (Department of Architecture ITS Selected Studio Projects 2018) and Editor-in-Chief for SKALA, the Department of Architecture ITS student magazine. Additionally, I have contributed to anthropological research on mid-20th-century Indonesian modernity, such as the Juragan Style architectural archive exhibition.

Education & Recognition

I hold a Bachelor of Architecture (BArch) from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), graduating with a GPA of 3.62/4.00. My work has been recognized through several awards, including being the 2nd Winner of the Krearture Series Vol. 4 (2021) and receiving the 2nd Prize in the Urbane Fellowship Program (2019).

Writing & Documentation

This platform serves as a space for me to document my thoughts and critical observations. You can find my further writings on architectural and cultural discourse on my Medium account, or view my professional journey on LinkedIn and Instagram.
