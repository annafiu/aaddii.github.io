About Adi Iman Wicaksono

I believe design should be critical, contextual, conscious, and efficient; above all, it must make sense.

I am an Architect at Tamara Wibowo Architects based in Semarang, Indonesia. My professional practice spans from early preliminary design proposals to overseeing construction projects, covering everything from interior elements to public building scales.

Beyond the Drafting Table

While my professional focus lies in architectural practice, I am equally passionate about the discourse surrounding architecture and art. I have extensive experience in critical writing, architectural publishing, and historical research.
